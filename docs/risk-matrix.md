# Risk Matrix

| Risk | Likelihood | Severity | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|----------|------------|
| Incorrect output | High | High | Restrict use to non-critical tasks |
| High latency | Medium | Medium | Use batching and caching |
| System failure | Low | High | Add monitoring and alerts |
| Drift over time | Medium | High | Monitor PSI and retrain |
| Misuse of output | Medium | Medium | Define clear usage guidelines |
